WIX:ReviewMe

: Idea generation : User Interface : Front-End Development

Summary

As a part of WIX hackathon I teamed up with software developer Diego and currently entrepreneur Yannick to come up with an idea of a widget for the WIX app market. We came up with a review aggregator to display customer reviews from sites like Google, Yelp or Facebook or Tripadvisor.

| About WIX hackathon

WIX is very simple and powerful website builder for people who would like to have a website without paying to developers and designers. WIX has very simple drag&drop interface and plenty of templates to choose from. It has it’s own app market where users can find many cool widgets and tools to integrate on their websites such as commenting widget, booking , social sharing widgets, weather or maps.

Problem

We focused mainly on small businesses who’s online visibility and promotion crucial to attract new customers and maintain sustainable business. But how do you attract new customers when there are so many business and just a click away?

Who are the users

Most of users are individual sellers or professionals who don’t have skills, budget and time to build the website themselves. Small businesses who have limited budget to pay for website being done by professionals.

Research and Brainstorming

There was no prior research done due to very tight time constrains. We did brainstorm ideas within our team.

Proposed Solution

For consumers reviews and recommendations are often the lead to decide whether they would like to use the service or not. It’s people’s voices and recommendation which brings faithful customers. People look at Google or Yelp to choose a restaurant because they can read what experiences people already had. Embed user feedback on your site through this review aggregator

Designs

The design should be generic and universal so it would fit on any style of a website. It should be easy to integrate with your wix site.

Winners

We have received good feedback from the judges and they offered us 500$ credit towards marketing on WIX app market.

Journey Continues ...

Getting the widget ready for the app market

Encouraged by the good feedback we have decided to continue on the prototype. There were few technical and legal challenges on the way: font-end integration to WIX app market, legality around icon and logo usage of review providers (Yelp, Google, Facebook)

I was contributing to font-end integration and custumisation on the app market. Wix has strong rules on keeping the styles and functionality of customasation. Wix is using angular fron-end framework.

The credit for majority of work goes to Diego who successfully finished the widget and you can find it here: Check out the widget on WIX https://www.wix.com/app-market/myreviews/overview .
