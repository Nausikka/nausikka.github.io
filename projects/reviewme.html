ReviewMe: Recommendation Widget

: Idea generation : User Interface : Front-End Development

Summary

As part of WIX hackathon I teamed up with software developer Diego and currently entrepreneur Yannick to develop a prototype of a widget for the WIX app market. We came up with a review aggregator to display customer reviews from sites like Google, Yelp or Facebook or Tripadvisor.

| About WIX hackathon

WIX is very simple and powerful website builder for people who would like to have a website without paying to developers and designers. WIX has very simple drag&drop interface and plenty of templates to choose from. It has it’s own app market where users can find many useful widgets to integrate on their websites such as commenting widget, booking, social sharing widgets, weather or maps.

Problem

We focused mainly on a small businesses who’s online visibility and promotion is necessary to attract new customers and maintain sustainable business. But how do you attract new customers when there are so many business and just a click away?

Who are the users

Most of users are individual sellers or professionals who don’t have skills, budget and time to build the website themselves.

Quickly sketched up profiles and needs of our typical users.

Research and Brainstorming

There wasn't any structured and systematic research done due to very tight time constrains (48 hours). We brainstormed ideas within our team. A lots of talking :)

There are many platfrom where people leaving reviews.

We explored reasoning in peoples behaviour when choosing a place to eat or have a coffee. Often customers reviews and recommendations is the trigger to decide whether we would like to use the service or not. People go to Google or Tripadvisor to choose a restaurant because they can read about others previous experiences.

Proposed Solution

A review aggregator that would provide trustworthy information from sites like Google, Yelp or Tripadvisor. The users can embed customers feedback in form of a widget on their websites.

Designs

The main consideration was customisability so the design would fit on any website style. So we kept designs very minimalistic and generic. We have used stars to reflect the level of satisfactory. As this is the most recognisable icons for the review used on google and yelp. We gave users to choose from three themes: dark, white and colour.

Winners

We have received good feedback from the judges and they offered us 500$ credit towards marketing on WIX app market.

Yannick, Me and Diego working hard

| Journey Continues ...

Getting the widget ready for the app market

Encouraged by the good feedback we have decided to continue on the prototype. There were few technical and legal challenges on the way: font-end integration to WIX app market, legality around icon and logo usage of review providers (Yelp, Google, Facebook)

I was contributing to font-end integration and custumisation on the app market. Wix has strong rules on keeping consistency of the styles and functionality of customasation. Wix is using Angular.js as fron-end framework.

The credit for majority of work goes to Diego Bizmate.biz who successfully finished the widget and you can find it on WIX app market: MyReviews.
