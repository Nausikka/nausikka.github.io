Climate Hack

: Brainstorming : Electronics

Summary

Climate Change is a big issue effecting everyone. It's necessary that people are aware of the concenqueces and understand. In collaboration with Cambridge Museums and Makespace we had 2 days to communicate what is happening with our planet is real.

Ideation

Looking for major climat effect happening currently on this planet. We were brainstorming possiblities how to link the museums niche character with climate change.

Brainstorming

Feedback

Feedback showed that simple and interactive ideas were the most feasable comparing with attractive and complex.

Prototyping

We were using only recycling item to keep the spirit of helping the planet. My contribution was in buiding the stand and connecting the LED light to simulate the sun ray.

Soldering

Audience Award

Visitors were excited to see how was the sun measured in the past and they could actually try it themselves. Climate change wouldn't be known without having historical data of measurements.
