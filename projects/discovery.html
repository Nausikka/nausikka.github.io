Discover: Genomic Data Search

: User Research : Prototyping : Usability Testing

Summary

Discovery is a web platfrom for searching human genomic data. It's serves research scientist find the relevant data for their research projects. It promotes ethical data reusability. And highlights importance of data sharing in scientific community.

This is caption to the image

Problem

Genomic data are scattered around the globe and often not searchable. Big potential for data resuability is hidden and takes lots of time to discover.

Other problem we have to deal with is conconsistency in the data structure and terminology. Currently search portals are oveloaded with information through which is very difficult to navigate.

Research

The research consisted from uncovering the current problems with data finding and sharing. The qualitative data was obtained form the interviews in both academia and industry environment.

Interviews were conduted to validate our hypothesis on what type of users searching for data the most. How the search strategy vary within different job role.

Surveys Interviews Use Cases
